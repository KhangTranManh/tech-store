Returns & Refunds Policy

Our Guarantee

At TechStore, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We stand behind the quality of our products and want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we offer a hassle-free return and refund policy.

Return Policy Overview

Most products can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. The following table outlines our specific return policies by product category:

Product Category Return Window Condition Requirements Special Notes
Laptops & Gaming PCs 30 days Unopened or like-new condition 15% restocking fee may apply if opened
Components (Graphics Cards, CPUs, etc.) 30 days Unopened or like-new condition Must include all original packaging and accessories
Monitors 30 days Unopened or like-new condition Dead pixel policy applies (see below)
Accessories 30 days Unopened or like-new condition No restocking fee
Software & Digital Products 14 days Unopened/unused No returns if product key has been activated
Custom Built PCs 30 days Like-new condition 20% restocking fee applies
Monitor Dead Pixel Policy: Monitors with 5 or more dead pixels or 3 or more clustered dead pixels are eligible for return or exchange within the return period.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Products returned beyond the 30-day return window
  • Products showing signs of physical damage, misuse, or unauthorized modifications
  • Opened software or digital products where the product key has been activated
  • Special order or custom-configured items (unless defective)
  • Clearance items marked as "Final Sale"

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

1
Start Your Return: Log in to your account and navigate to your order history, or contact our customer support team.
2
Request RMA Number: We'll provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return shipping instructions.
3
Pack Your Item: Securely package the item in its original packaging along with all accessories, manuals, and included items.
4
Ship Your Return: Attach the RMA label to the outside of the package and ship it back using the specified carrier.
5
Refund Processing: Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund. This typically takes 3-5 business days.
Important: Returns without an RMA number may be refused or experience significant processing delays. Always obtain an RMA number before returning any item.

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days after we process your return
  • PayPal: 1-2 business days after we process your return
  • Store Credit: Immediately after we process your return
  • Gift Cards: Refunded to the original gift card or as store credit

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (damaged item, wrong item shipped, etc.).

Exchanges

If you prefer to exchange an item rather than receive a refund, please contact our customer support team. Exchanges are subject to product availability and are processed as a return followed by a new order.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damage or a description of the defect
  3. We'll provide instructions for returning the item or may arrange for a pickup
  4. You'll receive a full refund including shipping costs or a replacement item

Need Help with a Return?

Our customer support team is here to assist with returns and refunds.
