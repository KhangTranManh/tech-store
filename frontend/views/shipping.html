Shipping Information

Shipping Methods

At TechStore, we offer several shipping options to meet your needs. All orders are processed and shipped from our warehouse within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 Business Days $9.99 (Free for orders over $50)
Express Shipping 2 Business Days $14.99
Next-Day Shipping 1 Business Day $24.99
International Shipping 7-14 Business Days Starting at $29.99 (varies by country)
Note: Next-Day and Express shipping options are only available for orders placed before 1:00 PM EST, Monday through Friday. Orders placed after 1:00 PM EST or on weekends will be processed the following business day.

Shipping Restrictions

Please be aware of the following shipping restrictions:

  • We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states and select international destinations.
  • Some large items (such as certain gaming PCs and monitors) may incur additional shipping charges due to size and weight.
  • We cannot ship to P.O. boxes for orders containing large items.
  • Certain products may not be eligible for international shipping due to regional restrictions.
  • International customers are responsible for any customs fees, taxes, and duties that may be charged upon delivery.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

  • Logging into your account and viewing your order history
  • Using our Order Tracking page with your order number and email address
  • Contacting our customer support team

Shipping Insurance

All orders are automatically insured up to $100 against loss or damage during shipping at no additional cost. For orders valued over $100, additional insurance is automatically added to your shipping cost.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any of the following issues with your delivery, please contact our customer support team within 48 hours of the delivery date:

  • Missing items in your package
  • Damaged items upon arrival
  • Package delivered to the wrong address
  • Significant delivery delays beyond the estimated delivery timeframe

Need Assistance with Shipping?

Our customer support team is here to help with any shipping questions or concerns.
