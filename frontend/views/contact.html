Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business day delivery. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination country. All shipping timeframes are estimates and may vary based on your location and product availability.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Items must be in their original packaging and in unused condition. Certain products, such as opened software or custom-built systems, may have different return policies. Please check the product page for specific return information or contact our customer support team.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes, we offer price matching for identical items sold by major retailers. The item must be in stock and available for immediate purchase from the competitor. To request a price match, please contact our customer support team with a link to the competitor's listing.

Can I cancel my order after it's been placed?

Orders can be canceled if they haven't entered the shipping process. To cancel an order, log into your account and go to your order history, or contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be canceled, but you can return it according to our return policy.

Do you offer technical support for products purchased?

Yes, we provide basic technical support for products purchased from TechStore. For complex issues, we may refer you to the manufacturer's technical support. Most products also come with manufacturer warranties that cover technical issues. For technical assistance, please contact our support team via phone or email.
